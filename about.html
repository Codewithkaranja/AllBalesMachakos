Our Story & Mission

Providing quality wholesale mitumba bales to retailers across East Africa since 2015. Your trusted partner in the second-hand clothing business.

Our Mission

To become East Africa's most reliable wholesale mitumba supplier by offering high-quality bales at competitive prices, supporting small businesses, and contributing to sustainable fashion through the circular economy.

Our Vision

To revolutionize the mitumba industry in East Africa by setting new standards for quality, transparency, and customer service while empowering thousands of retailers to grow their businesses.

Our Story

All Bales Machakos was founded in 2015 by Gloria Nzisa, who started with just a small stall in Machakos town. With a keen eye for quality and a deep understanding of the East African market, Gloria quickly built a reputation for providing some of the best mitumba bales in the region.

What began as a small-scale operation has grown into one of the most trusted wholesale suppliers in Machakos County and beyond. Today, we import high-quality second-hand clothing bales from the UK, Canada, China, and Scotland, serving retailers across Machakos, Makueni, Kitui, and the wider East African region.

Our success is built on strong relationships with our suppliers abroad and our loyal customers at home. We understand the unique needs of mitumba retailers and work tirelessly to provide bales that meet and exceed expectations.
