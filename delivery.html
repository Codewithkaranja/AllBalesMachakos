Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?

Once your order is dispatched, we'll send you a tracking number via WhatsApp. You can use this number to track your shipment with our courier partners. For urgent inquiries, you can also contact us directly on WhatsApp for updates.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, bank transfer, and cash on delivery (for local Machakos deliveries only). For regional orders, payment is required before dispatch via M-Pesa or bank transfer. We'll provide our payment details when you confirm your order.

Can I cancel or modify my order?

You can modify or cancel your order within 2 hours of confirmation, provided the order hasn't been processed for dispatch. Once processing has begun, changes may incur additional charges. Contact us immediately if you need to make changes to your order.

What happens if my bales are damaged during transit?

We pack all bales securely to prevent damage during transit. However, if your bales arrive damaged, please take photos immediately and contact us within 24 hours of delivery. We'll arrange for an inspection and work with you to resolve the issue, which may include replacement or compensation.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes! We offer tiered discounts for bulk orders. For orders of 10+ bales, you get a 5% discount. For 20+ bales, a 10% discount applies. For 50+ bales, contact us for special wholesale pricing. These discounts apply to the bale prices, excluding shipping costs.

How are shipping costs calculated?

Shipping costs are calculated based on your delivery zone, the number of bales ordered, and the total weight. The prices in our table are estimates per standard bale (approx. 45kg). For exact shipping quotes, please contact us with your order details and delivery address.
